The position

How to reach the apartment​

Airplane​

  • ​From Salerno-Costa d'Amalfi airport, reach the Montecorvino Rovella-Bellizzi station to reach the Salerno railway station by regional train.
  • From Naples Capodichino airport taking the Alibus shuttle and then from Naples Central Station the high-speed or regional train to Salerno.

​NB From Salerno train station, to reach Domus Fra' Giovanni, you can proceed on foot, taking about 15 minutes, or take a taxi (+39 089 757575).
For further information do not hesitate to contact us.

Train​

​​From all over Italy with Trenitalia and Italo high-speed trains, or alternatively regional trains, to Salerno railway station.
​From Salerno train station, to reach Domus Fra' Giovanni, you can proceed on foot, taking about 15 minutes, or take a taxi (+39 089 757575).
For further information do not hesitate to contact us.

Car​

​​The apartment is located in the center of Salerno, in a limited traffic area, so cars cannot enter without permission. For this reason we recommend leaving your car in one of the private or public car parks in the area, a 5-minute walk away from Domus Fra' Giovanni.
For further information do not hesitate to contact us.

Boat​

​By private boats or ferries at the following ports:

  1. ​Marina di Arechi (private moorings): to reach Domus Fra' Giovanni you can take a taxi (+39 089 757575), rent a car or take a bus to Salerno Center
  2. Porto Masuccio Salernitano (private moorings, ferries, hydrofoils): to reach Domus Fra' Giovanni you can proceed on foot, taking about 15 minutes, or take a taxi (+39 089 757575)
  3. Molo Manfredi-Maritime Station (private moorings, ferries, hydrofoils): to reach Domus Fra' Giovanni you can proceed on foot, taking about 10 minutes, or take a taxi (+39 089 757575)
